Refining your spec

  1. Find a partner, and answer afresh (as well as you can):
    • Who is the audience?
    • What does the audience value?
    • How will you know your project succeeded?
  2. With the same partner, spend the next hour sketching out the page(s) or workflows your user(s) will go through. You can check out these readings on spec-ing and sketching out a prototype if you're looking for some inspiration.
