Eight minutes to present

  1. You'll have 8 minutes…
  2. …tell us the story of your project; it's not just about the finished product.
  3. One suggestion if you're struggling to think through the layout of those eight minutes:
    • Who is the audience (totally cool if it's you!)?
    • What do you hope they'll get out of it?
    • Why was it an interesting project to you?
    • What can it do now?
    • What do you want it to be able to do next, eventually?
  4. If you know you want to present to the class or to us individually, email us right now.

open studio time

  • The rest of this class is going to be dedicated to getting you where you want to be with your final project(s).
  • If you would like one of us to sit and chat, consult, debug, or brainstorm with you before you can meaningfully get started this evening, please flag one of us down.
  • If you are still struggling to figure out the form your final project will take or when you should consider your project finished, check out this forum thread about different types of project specifications and demos.
  • Remember there are only two weeks left before final presentation, and one week until we're asking folks to have a 'functional' prototype ready to share as a demo.
