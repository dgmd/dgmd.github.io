Refining Letterpress

Added scoring, surrounding, and preventing the play of previously played words. Some suggested (and final, for the purely digital version) extensions:

  1. Add a more explicit indication which word was played previously.
  2. Add the ability to individually reset pieces and send them back to the board both by clicking and dragging them
  3. Add the ability to re-order and re-shuffle played tiles without needing to reset the entire tray.
  4. Add the ability to serialize the game state for communicating game changes over the network.

Figuring out your next steps

  1. Who is the audience?
  2. What are they supposed to feel, to get out of the project?
  3. What does the result look like?
  4. What's your plan for getting there? (Or at least, the next steps?)
  5. What are you stuck on or struggling with?
  6. If you could see a screencast of how to do one thing, what would you like to see?
