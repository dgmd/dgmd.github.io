Project Feedback

Don't let this happen to you!

If we were to begin our final projects at the beginning of the course, we would have more time to complete and perfect them. The final project changed everything for me, because I could get really passionate about solving problems on the way to achieving a vision I was excited about.
Now that I know the optional nature of everything, I would have started the final way earlier... it's hard to say.

Figuring out your next steps

  • Who is the audience?
  • What are they supposed to feel, to get out of the project?
  • What does the result look like?
  • What's your plan? (Or at least, the next steps?)
  • What are you stuck on or struggling with?
  • When could you sit down with us (or Skype)?
