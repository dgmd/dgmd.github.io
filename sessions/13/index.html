Where are you at with your final project?

Please write up the answers to these questions, and email us your answers and thoughts tonight before you leave.
  1. What are you working on? (e.g. What is your final project?) Fill us in as much as we need based on previous conversations.
  2. What are you struggling with? and/or What do you anticipate struggling with? Be as specific as you can. We will use your answers to this question to tailor the materials we provide over the next few classes.
  3. What is your plan for 'finishing' your final project by May 7? Please include a description of what you mean by 'finishing' -- e.g. what you are hoping to accomplish by the end of this class -- as well as week-by-week milestones that will get you there, keeping in mind:
    • Today: ~90 minutes of in-class project work-time.
    • April 30: ~90 minutes of in-class project work-time.
    • May 7: Penultimate class & small-group demos/crits.
    • May 14: Final class & project demos.
