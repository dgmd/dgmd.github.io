Tessel.io

  1. Try running tessel repl in Terminal. This will open a command line interface which allows you to interactively control the Tessel.
  2. Try toggling and otherwise controlling the four LEDs on the Tessel board from the REPL.
  3. Display the numbers 0–15 using the LEDs on the Tessel board.
  4. Light up an LED directly plugged into the Tessel.
  5. Control (4) by pressing a button.
  6. Use an LED as a light sensor and log its reading to the terminal at regular intervals.
  7. Blink the LEDs in (3) or (4) proportional to (6).
  8. Log out the readings from one of the modules that interest you in the terminal.
